# SawTank: Prison Break

A story-driven browser game inspired by *The Shawshank Redemption*. You play as **Saw Tank**, a tank who is wrongly convicted and sentenced to Shawshank State Prison. Navigate through courtroom, bus ride, yard, cellblock, and more as you befriend Red, face the challenges of prison life, and ultimately seek freedom.

## The Gist

Saw Tank is convicted of murder and sent to prison. Along the way, you'll meet Red—"the guy who can get things"—and other memorable characters. Walk through each scene, read the dialogue, and experience the story unfold. Will you find hope behind bars?

- **Controls:** Arrow keys or WASD to move • SPACE to advance dialogue
- **Style:** Cinematic 2D canvas with VCR-style visuals and atmospheric lighting
